About {Diana}

Diana Mahmoud has worked in digital media since the early 2000s, but understands the frustrations of those who feel overwhelmed by the computer age. “I understand not everyone grew up with an iPad because I didn't either. As much as I hate to admit it, I took a typing class instead of a keyboarding class. I also remember a time when a mouse was to be loathed, cookies were to be eaten, and browsing was done in public at the local mall.”

Despite this generational disadvantage, she built a career, first in traditional media and later in digital with a focus on customer engagement and online user experience. As an accomplished multimedia project manager with SAS’s award-winning Curriculum Pathways,a interactive educational software, she led the development of cutting-edge Internet content.

By the mid-2000s, she started exploring the ever-changing world of social media and begin teaching others who felt intimidated by the ever-changing digital world to have both the knowledge and confidence to improve their online presence and customer engagement. She uses her experience and expertise as a certified social media marketer and wed developer to help business owners understand how to use the latest tools to promote themselves and their businesses.

Are you ready to go social and engage your clients in the digital world? It’s time to stop feeling intimidated! Contact Diana to get it done!

Diana Mahmoud, Digital Media Consultant
